Which best describes your ethnicity?

Our ethnic heritages play a major role in determining the shapes of our bodies and weve found that by incorporating this information into Smart Sizes our algorithms are able to more accurately predict someones optimal dimensions.

This answer is only helpful when combined with other answers, and often only results in minor changes to the predicted dimensions. But those small changes are often the difference between a perfect fit and an imperfect fit.

This question is optional (as most are). Feel free to leave it blank if youre not comfortable answering or if you dont feel any of the options listed accurately describe your ethnicity.

Your answers to all questions are kept confidential, and are only used by Proper Cloth to calculate your Smart Size.
